Pickle Rick TryHackMe Room

Welcome to my first CTF writeup for the Pickle Rick Room on TryHackMe!

  • Back to TryHackMe Writeups

Intro

Welcome to my first ever CTF Writeup! I am writing this writeup to both help develop myself and my skills, whilst hopefully guiding others who are new to the field of Web Application Penetration Testing.

Please forgive any unusual styling or writing choices I make, as I develop my skillset! Without further ado, let's dive in the Pickle Rick room by TryHackMe!

In this room, we are given very little context:

  • Target IP: 10.130.170.42 (yours may differ)
  • We are attacking a web server
  • There are 3 flags to find

🔗 Link to Room: Pickle Rick - THM

Flag One

Initial enumeration using Nmap:

nmap -A -v 10.130.170.42

We discover ssh on port 22 and http on port 80.

Website discovered at http://10.130.170.42:80. Initial exploration didn’t provide further details.

Checking the HTML source revealed a commented username:

Username: R1ckRul3s

Using Gobuster to find directories:

gobuster dir -u http://10.130.170.42/ -w /usr/share/wordlists/dirbuster/directory-list-2.3-medium.txt -x php,html,txt

Login page accessed using credentials:

Username: R1ckRul3s
Password: Wubbalubbadubdub

First flag discovered via IDOR vulnerability:

Flag 1: mr. meeseek hair

Flag Two

After further enumeration, checking /home/rick revealed the second flag:

Flag 2: 1 jerry tear

Flag Three

Flag 3: fleeb juice

Summary

Flags obtained:

  1. mr. meeseek hair
  2. 1 jerry tear
  3. fleeb juice

We have effectively obtained the ingredients to turn Rick back into a human!
